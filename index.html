Welcome to Cobham Services,

This branch is part of a series of new openings in the county of Surrey, situated on the M25 between junctions 9 and 10. It was inaugurated on the 13th September 2012, and was designed to give customers the ideal space to relax, and allow them to enjoy an extensive line-up of food and retail outlets, which were made available, by the generous funding of Extra MSA Services Ltd, who is one of the fastest developing service station operator, that works widely and grows rapidly throughout various regions across UK.
